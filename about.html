About us

Telleria Construction Inc. is a commercial construction company serving the states of Cochabamba, Santa Cruz and La Paz in Bolivia. With over 7 years of experience we have earned our reputation for quality construction. Our project leaders utilize decades of field experience and key relationships to ensure that our projects are completed on time and under budget. Our goal is to make quality buildings for our clients. Every project we work on is fully backed with the complete support of the entire Telleria Construction team!

Marcelo W. Telleria is the CEO of Telleria Construction Inc. He started the company in 2013 making his first building of 5 floors with a capacity for 18 apartments. He named the building after his youngest daughter "Zoe". After making his first building he realized it was a good business so he started making more every year and always naming each building after a family member. Telleria Construction stands behind YOU!
